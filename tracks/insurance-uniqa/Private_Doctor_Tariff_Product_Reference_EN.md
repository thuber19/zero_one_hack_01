# UNIQA Private Doctor (Privatarzt) — Product Reference (In-Scope)

> **Scope note:** This document covers ONLY the Privatarzt ("Bei Arztbesuchen") path for "Ich selbst" (insurance for yourself only). The Krankenhaus (hospital) path, "andere Personen" (insurance for others), and advisor-required tariffs (Opt. Plus, Premium) are documented only as context — they route to advisor handoff and are **not** coached by the Conversion Coach.

**Source:** Überblick.pdf + individual tariff documents (QAYC S 2025, QAYC O 2025, QAZ FIT 2025, QAZ MW 2025, QAZ EW 2025)
**Tariff year:** 2025 | **Stand:** July 2025

---

## 1. Base Tariffs — Overview

The Privatarzt product line has **4 base tariffs**, differentiated by annual maximum amounts. Only Start and Optimal are online-purchasable.

| | **Start** | **Optimal** ✅ | **Opt. Plus** ❌ | **Premium** ❌ |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Tariff code | QAYC S 2025 | QAYC O 2025 | QAYC OP 2025 | QAYC P 2025 |
| Online purchase | **Yes** ✅ | **Yes** ✅ | No — advisor required | No — advisor required |
| Coach scopes this? | **Yes** ✅ | **Yes** ✅ | Routes to advisor | Routes to advisor |
| Risk assessment | Short questionnaire | Short questionnaire | Extended questionnaire | Full risk assessment |
| Annual maximum | €1,400 | €2,800 | €4,200 | €8,400 |
| Monthly premium (age 27) | **€38.74** | **€68.14** | €96.66 | €140.15 |
| Upgrade right | → Optimal (facilitated, no re-assessment) | → Opt. Plus or down → Start | → Premium or down → Optimal | → Down to Opt. Plus, Optimal, or Start |

❌ = Out of coaching scope. Visible in the calculator but routes to advisor if selected. The Coach should transparently explain this and redirect users toward Start/Optimal.

## 2. Base Tariff Breakdown — What's Covered and What's Not

### 2.1 Coverage Categories Per Tariff

| Coverage area | Start | Optimal ✅ | Opt. Plus ❌ | Premium ❌ |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **1. Doctor & specialist costs** (conv. & alternative medicine, telemedicine, diagnostics, outpatient surgery, preventive checkups) | €1,120 | €1,400 | €2,100 | €4,200 |
| **2. Medications, homeopathic remedies, prescription fees, vaccinations incl. travel vaccinations** | €280 | €560 | €840 | €1,680 |
| **3. Therapies & treatments** (physiotherapy, occupational therapy, psychotherapy, etc.) | — | €560 | €840 | €1,680 |
| **4. Medical aids & devices, visual aids / refractive eye surgery** (per 2 calendar years) | — | €280 | €420 | €840 |
| **Total annual maximum** | **€1,400** | **€2,800** | **€4,200** | **€8,400** |

### 2.2 Key Differences for the Conversion Coach

**Start vs. Optimal — the critical upgrade argument:**
- Start has **NO coverage** for therapies/treatments (Category 3) and **NO coverage** for medical aids, visual aids, or refractive eye surgery (Category 4)
- Optimal adds therapies (€560/year) and aids/vision (€280/year) — but costs ~€30/month more
- This is the primary "should I upgrade from Start to Optimal?" decision point — and a key Coach intervention opportunity

**Optimal vs. Opt. Plus vs. Premium — the "why not?" frustration point:**
- Opt. Plus and Premium offer higher coverage limits but are not online-purchasable
- Users clicking these see "advisory required" — this is the #1 frustration driver at Step 4 (66% drop-off)
- Coach intervention: transparently explain that these tariffs require a consultation, and highlight what Optimal already covers

## 3. What's Not Covered (All Tariffs)

The following are **excluded from all Privatarzt tariffs:**

- **Dental and oral surgery**, dental x-rays, dental prosthetics, orthodontics
- **Aesthetic/cosmetic treatments and operations** (unless to remedy accident consequences) — explicitly listed: abdominoplasty, breast augmentation/reduction/lift, liposuction, rhinoplasty, facelifts, etc.
- **Strength training and medical training** (even under medical supervision)
- **Memberships, subscriptions, and monthly fees** (gyms, etc.)
- **Psychotherapy** (covered under Optimal+ as clinical psychology/health psychology — but not standalone psychotherapy sessions)
- **Inpatient deductibles** (any deductible from a hospital/Sonderklasse tariff is never reimbursed)

## 4. How Reimbursement Works

### 4.1 Reimbursement Rate

| Scenario | Reimbursement |
|---|---|
| You have Austrian statutory health insurance (e.g., ÖGK) and it covers part of the cost | **100% of remaining costs** (total cost minus statutory insurance reimbursement) |
| You have statutory insurance but it covers nothing | **80% of costs** |
| You have no statutory health insurance | **80% of costs** |

### 4.2 How to Get Reimbursed

1. **If statutory insurance pays first:** Submit the statutory insurance reimbursement statement + copy of invoice to UNIQA via myUNIQA app/portal or by post
2. **If you submit to UNIQA first:** You cannot then submit the same invoice to statutory insurance
3. **Submission channels:** myUNIQA app/portal (photo/scan), or post to UNIQA Business Center 607, 1000 Vienna
4. **Deadline:** Claims expire after 3 years — submit promptly, especially thermal-paper receipts from pharmacies
5. **Per person:** Submit invoices separately for each insured person

### 4.3 Maximum Reimbursement Rule

Combined reimbursements from statutory health insurance + UNIQA cannot exceed **100% of actual costs**. You cannot profit from insurance.

## 5. Add-On Tariffs (Combinable with All Base Tariffs)

Three add-on tariffs can be combined with any Privatarzt base tariff. These are relevant for the Coach because they appear as optional extras during the online purchase flow.

| Add-on | Tariff code | Annual maximum | Monthly premium | Online-purchasable with Start/Optimal? |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Fit Feeling** | QAZ FIT 2025 | €280/year | €17.17/month | Likely yes ⚠️ |
| **Growing Mentally** | QAZ MW 2025 | €420/year | Not stated in docs | Likely yes ⚠️ |
| **Becoming Parents** | QAZ EW 2025 | €280/year | Not stated in docs | Likely yes ⚠️ |

⚠️ = Assumed online-purchasable when combined with Start/Optimal, but should be verified on the live calculator.

### 5.1 Fit Feeling (QAZ FIT 2025)

**What's covered** (80% of costs, up to €280/year total):
- Dietary supplements from pharmacies or medical dispensaries
- Massages by regulated trades (commercial massage, Shiatsu)
- Alternative treatments by free trades (incl. alternative practitioners / Heilpraktiker)
- At-home lab diagnostic tests
- Podology
- Back school (medical training)
- Smoking cessation programs
- **Fitness centers and fitness classes** (on-site or online) — up to €100/year sub-limit

**Minimum combination:** Must be combined with a UNIQA base tariff (any Privatarzt: Start, Optimal, Opt. Plus, or Premium). If the base tariff ends, this add-on also ends.

**Not covered:** Dental/oral surgery, cosmetic procedures, treatments related to alcohol/substance abuse.

### 5.2 Growing Mentally (QAZ MW 2025)

**What's covered** (80% of costs, up to €420/year total):
- Life & social counseling (Beratung, Coaching, Counselling, Betreuung) in:
  - Personal development
  - Self-discovery
  - Problem solving
  - Improving relationship skills
  - Professional supervision
  - **Psychosocial counseling** (with the explicit exclusion of psychotherapy)

**Minimum combination:** Must be combined with a UNIQA base tariff (any Privatarzt). If the base tariff ends, this add-on also ends.

**Not covered:** Psychotherapy sessions, dental/oral surgery, cosmetic procedures, treatments related to alcohol/substance abuse.

**Coach note:** This is an important differentiator — "Growing Mentally" covers life coaching and psychosocial counseling but explicitly **not** psychotherapy. Users expecting therapy coverage may be disappointed.

### 5.3 Becoming Parents (QAZ EW 2025)

**What's covered** (80% of costs, up to €280/year total):
- Pregnancy tests
- Outpatient midwife services (care, counseling, support during birth, maternity & infant care)
- Breastfeeding counseling, postnatal exercise courses
- Family support for the first 8 weeks after birth (cleaning, meals, childcare)
- Nutritional counseling (by regulated trade)
- Outpatient treatment of chronic wounds

**Important condition:** Benefits under "Family planning" (items b–d) are **only paid if this add-on was already in force at the time pregnancy began**. If you buy it after becoming pregnant, family planning benefits are not available.

**Minimum combination:** Must be combined with a UNIQA base tariff (any Privatarzt). If the base tariff ends, this add-on also ends.

## 9. Upgrade / Conversion Rights

This is a **critical Coach argument** for users hesitating between Start and Optimal, or wanting Opt. Plus/Premium:

| From | Upgrade to | Conditions |
|---|---|---|
| Start → | Optimal | **Facilitated conversion**: no renewed health assessment, no new waiting periods. Requirements: (1) minimum 3 years in current tariff, (2) not yet 65 years old, (3) request received ≥1 month before renewal date |
| Optimal → | Opt. Plus | Same facilitated conversion conditions |
| Optimal ↓ | Start | Permitted, same 3-year minimum |
| Opt. Plus → | Premium | Same facilitated conversion conditions |
| Opt. Plus ↓ | Optimal | Permitted, same 3-year minimum |
| Premium ↓ | Opt. Plus, Optimal, or Start | Permitted, same 3-year minimum |

**Minimum insurance duration after any conversion:** 3 years.

**Coach argument:** "Not sure about Optimal? Start with Start — you can upgrade to Optimal after 3 years without a new health check. Your premium will adjust to your age at that time, but your health status won't be re-assessed."

## 10. Option (Waiver of Premium) — Supplementary Coverage

Available for all base tariffs:

| Feature | Details |
|---|---|
| Premium | 15% of the monthly base tariff premium |
| Coverage during option | **None** — no benefits are paid while the option is active |
| Maximum entry age | 35 |
| Reactivation | Annually at renewal date, up to age 50 |
| What it does | If you become unable to work (illness/disability), the option covers your insurance premium payments so your coverage continues |

## 13. Annual Maximum Calculations — What You Actually Get

For coaching, it helps to frame coverage in terms a user understands:

**Start (€38.74/month = €464.88/year):**
- You pay €464.88/year and can claim up to €1,400/year
- But: no therapies, no medical aids, no vision coverage
- Best for: people who only need doctor visits and medications

**Optimal (€68.14/month = €817.68/year):**
- You pay €817.68/year and can claim up to €2,800/year
- Adds: therapies (€560), medical aids/vision (€280), more medications (€560 vs. €280)
- Best for: people who want comprehensive outpatient coverage
- **3.4x coverage-to-premium ratio** compared to 3.0x for Start
